4C Family Child Care Registry for Ohio Providers

Being included on the 4C Family Child Care Registry means that your family child care business meets 4C's 20 minimum standards for a family child care provider. There are many advantages to being on this free child care registry:

  • You will be included in our child care data base so that when parents call us looking for child care, we can make free referrals to you when a parent’s needs match what you have to offer in terms of ages/hours/days. Over 8,500 families contacted 4C for help finding child care last year!
  • You will be placed on our mailing list to receive information on trainings such as first aid, CPR and health and safety—as well as help getting started on your CDA!

You also become eligible to participate in a special Provider Insurance Program. Benefit plans include:

  • medical insurance
  • dental insurance
  • accident expense insurance
  • disability insurance
  • prepaid legal
  • liability insurance for child care business

It’s easy to be included on the 4C Registry. In Southwest Ohio, call Dawn Hoskins at 513-758-1235 or 800-256-1296 x1235. In the Miami Valley, call Mary Hale at 937-723-2727 or 800-256-1296 x2727.
